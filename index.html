User Demographic Persona: Building Supply Site

John Smith-Site Manager

John is looking for a quicker way to order materials to be delivered to his job site and thinks that a web order may be just the thing. However, John has worked in construction his whole life and like many in that industry, has no technical/computer skills. John needs clear text and links around a home page to help him navigate the site.
